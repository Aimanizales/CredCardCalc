Why should you attend?

If you or a loved one is new to Medicare this year, you're sure to have questions about how Medicare works and how to pick a plan that best meets your needs.

PrimeCare is dedicated to quality health care for San Bernardino County, California residents. We are pleased to offer this one-hour program to help you understand Medicare and answer your questions.

Plus, we'll introduce you to the unique benefits of our Aetna Medicare Prime Plan (HMO).

Here are just a few more key benefits you'll enjoy with the plan:

  • $0 copay for primary care physician and specialist office visits
  • Preventive dental allowance: $500 every year
  • Expanded drug coverage
  • Access to doctors and hospitals you know and trust, such as PrimeCare, Desert Valley Medical Group and St. Mary High Desert Medical Group
